Our Procedure

We aim to provide an integrated Hospital environment between a doctor and a patient in a hospital.

A patient can register and create his/her profile with a brief description of the health problems. A specialist would be alloted based on the symptoms. After the analysis of patient's symptoms through consultation, the patient's reports and prescription would be uploaded to his profile. He would also be alerted through text messages about his medical prescriptions. Patients would have access to Conference videos and also would be provided with health tips.
